Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

A standard kindergarten curriculum focuses on foundational skills: basic literacy, numeracy, social interaction, and creative exploration.

Elementary School

Elementary education develops literacy, math, science, and social studies. It fosters problem-solving, teamwork.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school curriculum deepens subject knowledge in language arts, math, sciences, and history. It incorporates critical thinking.
